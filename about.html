About The Author

Hi, I'm Giang. And I love bakery.

Bakery is my dream in the future. I always want to discover many different recipes for the different kinds of cake. Whenever I have free time, I will spend the whole day baking and finding new recipes. Tasting diferent kinds of cakes always makes my day.

Chocolate cookie is the cookie I like best. I love the mixture between the sweetness of cookie and the bitterness of chocolate. It is the best taste I have ever had in my life. During the Christmas, I often make lots of chocolate cookies for my family and my parents. Seeing people smile when they eat my cookies satisfies me a lot.
